Q1
Is this safe for my LinkedIn account?
Yes. UTG Labs respects LinkedIn's weekly limits (100 connection requests per week) and uses your real LinkedIn session — no scraping, no shady automation. Accounts with 500+ existing connections and standard activity can run at full speed from day one.

Most recruiters see their first replies within 3–5 days of launching a campaign. A typical outreach campaign generates 20–40 conversations per month at around a 30% acceptance rate.
